I am a B.Arch candidate at the New Jersey Institute of Technology with a focus on adaptive reuse, urban regeneration, and socially responsive design.

My work combines rigorous technical execution with conceptual depth—using site history, environmental analysis, and narrative to produce architecture that is both functional and meaningful.

I believe architecture is a collective act of responsibility, and I am committed to designing spaces that inspire connection and endure over time.
